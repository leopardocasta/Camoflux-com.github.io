[ ABOUT ]

A playable installation. Fiberglass furniture, vinyl, custom controllers, ultra-high-definition video game. Camoflux: Incendio Igapó is presented alongside a 360° in-game video capture and the artport commission Camoflux: Recall Grotto.

[ WORKS ON VIEW ]
03 INSTALLATIONS
WORK_01

Camoflux: Levels & Bosses — Incendio Igapó

2023–26 · Ultra-high-definition video game · fiberglass furniture · vinyl

The central installation. A playable chapter of Camoflux presented as spatial encounter. Visitors take turns at the controllers; the rest watch.

CAMOFLUX: LEVELS & BOSSES — INCENDIO IGA
WORK_02

Camoflux Incendio Igapó 360

2026 · 8K 360° video · custom software · 8:10

An in-game video capture rendered at full resolution. Adjacent to the playable work, it stages the same spatial material under different terms — the camera logic of world-recording.

CAMOFLUX INCENDIO IGAPÓ 360
WORK_03

Camoflux Recall Grotto

2025–26 · Browser-based game · commissioned for artport · acquired into Whitney collection

A separate, browser-playable work. As an organic drone, gather water and sunlight to cultivate cyberflora across a primordial landscape. Holographic memories surface from the blooming vegetation.
